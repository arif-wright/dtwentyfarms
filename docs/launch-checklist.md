# Launch checklist

## Business and content

- [ ] Confirm business name, operator name, public email, canonical domain, and public pickup-area wording
- [ ] Obtain final logo system, favicon, packaging mark, original crop/product photography, and grower portrait
- [ ] Approve prices, quantities, variety rotation, per-customer limits, minimum/maximum capacity, dates, and pickup window
- [ ] Replace every demonstration date and label; confirm whether counts remain hidden
- [ ] Review product handling, labeling, allergen, food-safety, cottage-food/business, tax, and local regulatory requirements
- [ ] Have privacy policy and terms reviewed; add effective dates and contact method

## Commerce and operations

- [ ] Create and verify owned Square product/payment links; test success, cancel, receipts, refunds, and mobile handoff
- [ ] Document manual threshold evaluation, reconciliation, notification, refund, cutoff, sold-out, delay, and cancellation procedures
- [ ] Confirm exact-address disclosure occurs only after an eligible confirmed order
- [ ] Test customer-arranged courier handoff and missed-pickup policy
- [ ] Connect real email signup and contact providers with consent, validation, spam controls, retention, and error handling

## Technical

- [ ] Set production URL/environment, TLS, logs, monitoring, backups, and uptime checks
- [ ] Run lint, check, unit, build, and Playwright suites
- [ ] Test 320, 375, 768, 1024, and 1440px; keyboard, screen reader basics, contrast, reduced motion, and no horizontal overflow
- [ ] Validate metadata, structured data, sitemap, robots, social previews, CSP, security headers, and 404s
- [ ] Confirm no secrets, private address, phone, customer data, internal metrics, or misleading claims exist in source/build output
